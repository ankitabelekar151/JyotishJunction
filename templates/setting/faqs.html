Which types of questions am I allowed to ask?

You have the liberty to ask us about any aspect of your life. We suggest referring to our "Customize Your Experience" section and selecting a question from the sample questions provided for personalized insights.

Is it possible to ask multiple questions simultaneously?

Depending on the package you choose, you can ask questions accordingly. We offer three packages with different benefits:
1. "Stellar Insights" - This package provides a comprehensive astrology consultation where you can ask a single question.
2. "Celestial Guidance" - With this package, you'll receive an in-depth astrology consultation allowing you to ask up to 5 questions (for a single user only). All 5 questions must be asked within a 30-day period.
3. "Divine Revelations" - Our premium package offers an extensive astrology consultation for a family (up to 4 users), including 10 questions and an optional 30-minute video call. The primary user can share the questions from their login, providing the details of the remaining 3 users (including date/time/place/country of birth). All 10 questions and the video call must be utilized within 30 days.

In case I have a follow-up question, how can I proceed
with asking it?

If you have a follow-up question, please feel free to ask a new question based on the package that suits your requirements. The new question will be assigned to the same expert who handled your previous question.

Can I be assured that the astrologers / experts

Absolutely! All the astrologers on the Jyotish Junction platform undergo a meticulous verification process. We verify their documents and conduct a screening test to ensure their authenticity. Rest assured, all our experts are verified and possess significant experience in their respective fields.

Should i share my contact details to the

No, if you value your privacy, you should not share your contact details such as email address, phone number, or address with the astrologer.

Are the astrologers aware of the identity of the person they are interacting with?

No, the astrologers do not have any access to your email, phone number, or address at any given time.

What is the procedure for accessing my past questions and chat conversations?

To view your consultation details, go to the "My Profile" section and click on "My History." You will find all the pertinent information, including the transcript of your chat, emails, and more. If you wish to retain them, you can choose to keep them, or if desired, select the delete option to remove them from your history.
